  OR
  $ node server.js
  Then open: http://localhost:3000

🎮 HOW TO PLAY
  • Move mouse to control your cell
  • Eat red orbs to grow (+1 size)
  • Eat smaller players to grow more (+50% of their size)
  • Must be 1.1x larger to eat another player
  • Avoid larger players!
  • Maximum 5 players

🔧 CONFIGURATION
  Edit GAME_CONFIG in server.js:
  • WORLD_WIDTH/HEIGHT: Game world size
  • NUM_ORBS: Number of orbs
  • MAX_PLAYERS: Player limit (1-5)
  • UPDATE_RATE: Game speed
  • BASE_SPEED: Movement speed
